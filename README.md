# 🌌 Decentralised Low Earth ORBIT STM

---

> **Open-Source**

---

## 📖 Overview

A decentralised, peer-to-peer collision avoidance protocol designed for **Low Earth Orbit (LEO) mega-constellations** — where satellites operate collectively as a unified system to provide global coverage.

---

## 🚀 Key Objectives

- 🛰️ Enable **autonomous satellite coordination**
- 🔄 Facilitate **real-time trajectory negotiation**
- 🌐 Support **distributed orbital maneuvers**
- ❌ Eliminate reliance on **ground-based intervention**

---

## ⚠️ Problem Addressed

### Kessler Syndrome
A scenario where space debris increases exponentially due to collisions, threatening the sustainability of space operations.

---

## 💡 Solution Approach

- 🤝 Peer-to-peer satellite communication
- ⚡ Real-time decision-making algorithms
- 🧠 Autonomous maneuver execution
- 🌍 Fully decentralised architecture

---

## 🎯 Impact

- Reduces risk of **orbital collisions**
- Enhances **space sustainability**
- Enables scalable deployment of **mega-constellations**
- Strengthens resilience through **distributed control**
